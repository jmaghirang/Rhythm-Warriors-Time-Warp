
--------------------
Japanese Old Town
--------------------


Description:
--------------------
This package contains 3d assets to construct your Japanese Old Town (in the Edo period).


Content
--------------------
- Japanese Castle
- Japanese Shrine
- Japanese Old Houses(*Only 3 houses have a simple room.)
- Props
- Nature

- 2 Demo scenes
- 221 FBX
- 209 Prefabs
- 2 textures(type:albedo map/size:1024x1024)

Triangle Count
----------------------
Castle: 20000 - 3000
Shrine: 5000 - 500
House: 2800 - 400
Tree: 800 - 130
Mountain: 310 - 80
Ground: 400 - 50
Prop: 1600-30

Some asset (Building) has  low polygon model asset. 

Info
--------------------------
*This package contains only 3d art, no scripts or musics.
*All screenshots and videos are captured on PC build.
